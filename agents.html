Subscription Coding Agents

Plan pricing for agent products that bill per seat / per month (not per token). Sortable cheapest → most expensive.

Why this is a separate list: these products are billed by subscription, so they can't be ranked on a $/token basis like the live model table. Prices here are curated (verified by hand against each vendor's page) and auto-checked weekly — a scheduled job re-reads each pricing page and opens a pull request when something changes, so a human can confirm the new numbers. Always click through to the source before relying on a price.

Notes: prices are US list prices in USD per month — your local price may differ by currency, VAT, and region. Paid plans are per seat unless shown as Usage-based. Allowance is each vendor's own unit (premium / AI requests, messages, usage credits, or ×multipliers) — not tokens, and not comparable across vendors; figures tagged est. are best-effort and unconfirmed. Reset is the allowance window (monthly vs rolling). A Unverified badge means the price still needs a manual pass (the weekly monitor flags changes but never overwrites values automatically). Use the Individual / Business toggle to switch between personal plans and team/enterprise plans.

Authoritative data lives in data/agents.json; the weekly monitor writes detected price signals to data/agents.snapshot.json. Codex CLI has no standalone subscription — it's bundled with ChatGPT plans or billed per token via the API (see the model table).
